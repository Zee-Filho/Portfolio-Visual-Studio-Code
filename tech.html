KORE GALLERY

Reformer Pilates

Kore Gallery was the first LGBTQI + Pilates Studio located in East London that focuses on helping people develop a new life style by improving their body awareness, posture and muscular strength, and it also focuses on diversity. We believe everyone should have access to our fitness workouts on the reformer.


Pilates has been proven to realign our bodies and prevent injuries, as it involves low-impact workouts through movements that target specific muscles, benefiting people of all ages and fitness levels.


Beyond developing a strong and powerful core, we also focus on the social aspect of our sessions, because we want clients to interact with one other, creating a sense of community.
